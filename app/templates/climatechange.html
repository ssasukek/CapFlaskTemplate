Climate Change

Climate Change is the shifts in the temperature and weather patterns that can affect many places and make different living conditions very hard to live in. It doesn't just affect animals, but every living organism in the world is affected by this well known cause. Many Habitats or places people and organisms live in are very hard due to resources such as food or water is available due to climate change.

Hows Does Climate Change affect Pandas?

Climate Change can possibly remove habitats from Pandas making it harder for them to live and find a home. It can also reduce their food supply and water supply causing pandas to force to move or possible die within the conditions they are currently living in.
